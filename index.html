Course We Offer

We are offer to three best computer science course for student by Gokul Global University

CSE

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), RGMCET, Nandyal, was established in the year 1995 with a modest intake of 40 students. Over the years it has grown by leaps and bounds and presently offers the B.Tech course in Computer Science and Engineering with a sanctioned intake of 240 students.

ECE

The Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) Department has been established in the year 1995. The department offers UG Engineering –B. Tech and (UG), PG Engineering – M. Tech that provide students with the knowledge and skills they need to succeed in the Electronics and Communication Engineering.

MCA

P.G. course in Master of Computer Applications (MCA) with an intake of 60. The Department has been recognized as a Research Centre to offer Full Time Doctoral Program in Computer Science and Engineering, under the affiliation of Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University.

MBA

The Department of Management Studies was started in the year 2006 in Rajeev Gandhi Memorial college of Engineering and Technology with an initial intake of 60 students for post-graduation in Business Management (MBA), under the affiliation of Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University.
